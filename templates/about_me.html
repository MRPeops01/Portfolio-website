How my coding journey started

My coding journey started in 2nd grade when my parents had a surprise for me and my brother.

We arrived at a place called Code Ninjas, where we could learn how to code.

After the first class, we loved it and kept going.

We had to stop though when I started playing basketball; I was an orange belt when I stopped.

When we were on a travel team, one of my brother's teammate's dad knew a lot about coding.

He told me to look at courses on Udemy, where you could take courses on anything.

When I took my first Python course, I loved it and decided that I wanted to be a software and web developer.
